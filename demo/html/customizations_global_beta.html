Global Customizations

When you've configured your own app, you have the option of tweaking the default look and feel of all the trNgGrid instances.

Almost all the templates and the CSS classes, together with other global options, can be configured at start time. You can customize these the moment you configure your app:

You can find all the templates in beta/src/js/trNgGridConfiguration.ts. Find your inspiration there when you wish to override the default look and feel of the grid.

As you'll be marrying your templates with the inner guts of the component, you'll have to check and adjust your custom templates whenever you upgrade.
